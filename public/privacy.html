eGeez - Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

eGeez is an offline alphabet-learning game for children, made by PromiseChain. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Ever.

What the app collects

Nothing. eGeez has no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party trackers. The app works fully offline after the first download and makes no network calls of its own.

Where progress lives

A child's learning progress (letters learned, stars, streaks, settings) is stored only on the device, in the app's local storage. It never leaves the device unless a grown-up explicitly exports it with the "Move to another phone" feature, which saves a file the grown-up controls.

Sharing features

Share cards, challenge links, and voice postcards are created on the device and go only where the grown-up sends them, through the phone's own share sheet. Nothing is uploaded to us - we have no servers.

Microphone

The microphone is used only inside the optional Family Voice recorder, an adult flow behind the parental gate. Recordings stay on the device and leave it only inside a file the adult explicitly chooses to share. The app never requests the microphone outside that flow.

Children's privacy

eGeez is designed for children and complies with children's privacy law (COPPA and GDPR-K) the simplest way possible: by collecting nothing at all. Adult-only areas (parents dashboard, purchase links, sharing) sit behind a parental gate.

Changes

If a future version ever adds an optional online feature, this policy will be updated first and the feature will be off by default and behind the parental gate.
