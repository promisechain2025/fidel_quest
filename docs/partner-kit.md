# Community Partner Kit — churches, weekend schools, community groups

> Phase 1 of `community-teacher.md`. How a community earns by bringing families,
> with no backend and no child data. Hand this (adapted) to a partner.

## The idea

Your church / weekend school / community group brings families to Fidel Quest,
and gets **credit toward free family licenses** (or a simple manual payout) for
the families who join through you. Families keep everything private — this is
just an attribution tag, no child data leaves any device.

## How a family credits you

1. A grown-up opens **Backpack → Grown-ups** (a quick hold-and-tap gate).
2. Under **Community code**, they type your code (e.g. `DEBRE`) and tap **Apply**.
3. That's it — your group is credited for that family.

Your **code** is short, uppercase letters/numbers (max 12), easy to say aloud
in a hall or print on a flyer. Pick something memorable: `DEBRE`, `SELAM`,
`ASMARA2026`.

## The two paid paths (choose per partner)

**A. Bulk / sponsored licenses (simplest, store-native).**
- **iOS:** buy a batch of gifts (or use Apple Volume Purchase); hand each family
  a redemption link. Each is spent on first redeem.
- **Android:** we generate **Play promo codes** (single-use); we send you a code
  sheet to distribute.

**B. Affiliate credit.**
- Families enter your **community code** (above). We tally how many joined
  through you and credit you — the clean default is **free seats** you can give
  to more families (stays inside store mechanics, no payout plumbing). A manual
  quarterly cash payout is possible once volume justifies the bookkeeping.

## What we track (and what we don't)

- **Tracked (only if the family's app has analytics on):** an anonymous
  `community:<CODE>` count. That's the whole attribution signal.
- **Never tracked:** the child, their name, their progress, their location, or
  anything that identifies a family. No accounts. The published kids app can
  still declare **no data collected**.

## Store rules (short version)

- App payment always flows through Apple/Google — we can't sell a "paid link"
  ourselves. That's why the paid paths above are gifts / promo codes / Volume
  Purchase.
- Sharing a cut of in-app subscription revenue with a partner is allowed, but it
  comes out of our net after the 15–30% store fee — which is why **free seats**
  is the friction-free starting point.

## Getting set up (partner checklist)

- [ ] Pick your **code** and tell us — we note it so credits are attributed.
- [ ] Choose a paid path (bulk licenses, promo codes, or affiliate credit).
- [ ] Get your **flyer line**: *"Learn the Fidel with Anbessa — enter code `DEBRE`
      in Grown-ups → Community code."*
- [ ] (Later) When there's demand, we open the **teacher dashboard** tier
      (`community-teacher.md`, Phase 2) so you can follow each child's progress —
      that step adds parental consent + a class code.
